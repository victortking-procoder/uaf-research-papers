# Paper 1

## An AI-Powered Early Warning System for Predicting Infrastructure Failure in Remote Arctic Communities

This paper presents a hybrid LSTM + Isolation Forest framework for early warning in remote Arctic infrastructure systems. It focuses on predictive maintenance under constraints such as sparse data, intermittent connectivity, limited local technical capacity, and the high operational cost of failure. The study evaluates the system on a blended dataset strategy and proposes a phased deployment path for real-world adoption.

**Keywords:** predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, early warning systems, Arctic infrastructure, edge-aware ML
